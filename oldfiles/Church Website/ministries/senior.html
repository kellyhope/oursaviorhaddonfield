Senior Ministry Volunteer Opportunities

Senior Activities/Trips volunteers coordinate bus trips and activities for seniors at LCOS, such as visits to museums, Radio City Music Hall in New York City, and the Reading Terminal Market in Philadelphia.

Senior Game Day volunteers plan, purchase, and prepare food for a gathering of local seniors for lunch, games and fellowship. The event is free, but participants are encouraged to bring a donation of a canned good for the LCOS pantry. Senior Game Day is held the last Tuesday of each month at noon.
