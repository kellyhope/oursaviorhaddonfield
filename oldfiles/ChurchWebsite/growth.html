Opportunities for Spiritual Growth

First Holy Communion is celebrated each spring by sixth graders who have completed instruction in the Sacrament.

Affirmation of Baptism (Confirmation) is made each May by eighth graders who have completed the two year course of instruction for Confirmands.

Bible Study meets on Monday evenings and Wednesday mornings during eight week sessions in the fall and early spring. All are welcome to attend.

New Member Classes are scheduled periodically for those considering becoming members of Our Savior. The three-session classes focus on the history and beliefs of Lutherans and provide an orientation to the ministry of Our Savior.

Adult Forums are informal study groups which meet each Sunday at 9:00 a.m. in the church parlor to discuss Christian teachings and social concerns.

Magonigal Lectures are a two-week series of free lectures offered on Monday evenings in the spring, funded by a memorial gift from the estate of Helen Magonigal.
