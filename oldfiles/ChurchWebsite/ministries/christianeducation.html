Christian Education Volunteer Opportunities

Adult Forum Leaders plan and host discussions or videotape forums for adults on topics including Christian teachings and social concerns.

Church Library Volunteers catalog, shelve and check books, videotapes, magazines and related materials.

Holiday Program Assistants help with holiday pageants, musical performances, crafts and activities for the Sunday School children as needed.

Lenten Program Assistants help with five weekly Lenten Soup Night dinners as needed. Volunteers serve on a team, which is responsible for preparing food, setting up tables in Parish Hall, serving and clean up after the dinner.

Sunday School Teachers and Substitutes review course materials, plan, and teach a class of children, age four through grade six, about our faith. Sunday School Helpers assist the teachers with lessons or activities.

Vacation Bible School Teachers provide a Christian education program for children from three years old through fourth grade, half-days for one week in the summer. Vacation Bible School Assistants assist the teachers and volunteers with lessons, crafts, music, drama and recreation. A Crafts Coordinator plans, purchases material for, and implements creative activities for Vacation Bible School. A Recreation Coordinator plans and supervises a recreation program for the Bible School students. A Snack Coordinator plans, purchases, and serves themed snacks for the children.
