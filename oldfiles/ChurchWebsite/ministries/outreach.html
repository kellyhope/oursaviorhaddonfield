Outreach Volunteer Opportunities

Children’s Church volunteers plan and assist the pastor in conducting a special pre-school friendly worship service for parents and children the third Wednesday of each month. The service begins at 7:00 p.m. and is one-half hour long.

College Care Package volunteers prepare packages of treats, phone cards, The Messenger, and church Bulletins and send them twice a year to members living away at college. The program is supported by donations from the congregation.

The Cookie Patrol makes and delivers a gift of cookies to visitors and invites them to return.

Greeters greet worshippers and assist visitors as they arrive at the ground floor entrance lobby for Sunday services.
